Identify Your Behavorial Patterns!

As we pay close attention, we can spot some behavioral patterns in ourselves and others. These behavioral patterns make us perform exceptionally well in certain situations and make it difficult for us in certain other situations.

Researchers have long studied and classified these patterns. When we develop the awareness of our behavioral patterns, our capability to play in the environment gets better. We are able to deal with external situations tactfully, and we get better insights of

  • What to engage with
  • When to ask for help
  • What to avoid

This helps us in taking actions that are aligned with who we are. Thus we end up feeling better, as there are fewer conflicts
